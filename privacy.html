Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use and safeguard your information.

1. Introduction

DC Performance Coaching ("we", "our" or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and protect the information you provide when you visit our website, book consultations or purchase programmes.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Contact Information: such as your name, email address and telephone number when you submit a form, book a consultation or contact us.
  • Usage Data: details of your visits to our website, including traffic data, location data and other communication data.
  • Technical Data: your IP address, browser type, operating system and other technical details collected through analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you request.
  • Schedule and manage consultations.
  • Deliver and improve our programmes, products and services through analytics and user feedback.
  • Comply with our legal obligations and enforce our terms and conditions.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to personalise your experience, analyse traffic and remember your preferences. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie usage through your browser settings. We only set non‑essential cookies after obtaining your consent via the cookie banner.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure or access.

6. Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data. You may also have the right to portability of your data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
