About DC Performance Coaching

Decades of athletic experience meet cutting‑edge sports science.

Founded by multi‑sport athlete and coach Daniel Courtney, DC Performance Coaching delivers high‑end strength & conditioning, hypertrophy and body composition programmes. Our mission is simple: empower you to move better, grow stronger and perform at your peak through evidence‑based training and true one‑to‑one support.

We work with youth athletes, busy professionals, weekend warriors and elite performers across Ireland and beyond. Whether you're chasing a new personal best or rebuilding after injury, our personalised approach gets results.
